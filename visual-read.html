The Clever Fox and the Moonlit Bridge

Source: Teacher Uploaded Text

One quiet night, a fox came to a river. He wanted to cross to the other side, but the was narrow and the water below rushed quickly.

The fox sat down and thought for a while. "If I run, I might fall," he .

Then he looked up at the moon. "I will be and go slowly."

The fox stepped carefully onto the bridge. Halfway across, the bridge creaked, but he kept going.

He stopped, took a deep breath, and kept going. At last, he reached the other side.

He looked back and smiled. "I made a to myself, and I kept it."

From that night on, the fox knew that being clever is good, but being brave and keeping a promise is even better.
