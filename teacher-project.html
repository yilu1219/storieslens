Story Bible

Keep characters, settings, and visual style consistent across the whole class project.

Project Title

The Mystery of the Lost Map

Story Premise

A group of students discover an old map in the school library and follow clues around the campus.

Main Characters

Mia, Leo, Ava, Noah, and Emma. Students may write actions and dialogue, but not redesign characters.

Main Settings

School library, playground, old garden tree, hidden door, and moonlit classroom finale.

Visual Style

Warm storybook illustration, soft colors, cinematic classroom adventure, no readable text, no logos.

Tone

Curious, safe, mysterious, hopeful, and classroom-friendly.

Do

  • Keep characters consistent.
  • Use school-friendly visuals.
  • Use the approved character reference images.

Don't

  • Do not change character age, clothing, hairstyle, or face style.
  • Do not include real student names in images.
  • Do not add logos or readable text.
